# Privacy Policy

**Gitsugi** is a Chrome extension that applies a cosmetic gold effect to empty cells on GitHub contribution graphs. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What data is stored

The extension uses `chrome.storage.sync` to save your preferences:

- Enabled/disabled state
- Profile scope (all profiles or own profile only)
- Shimmer direction
- Shimmer speed
- Replay delay
- Gold intensity

These settings are synced through your Chrome profile and never leave your browser. No other data is stored.

## What data is collected

None. Gitsugi does not collect, track, or transmit any information about you, your browsing activity, or the pages you visit.

## Third-party services

Gitsugi does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services. All code runs locally in your browser.

## Host permissions

The extension requires access to `github.com` solely to inject CSS and JavaScript that modify the visual appearance of the contribution graph. No page content is read, recorded, or sent anywhere.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue at https://github.com/johnrbell/gitsugi/issues.
